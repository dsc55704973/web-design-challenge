WEATHER OR NOT

Welcome! This is a simple site for hosting my data visualizations for a simple weather analysis per city.

In a series of four models, we have a clear visualization of how certain weather aspects change the closer we get to the equator. These "weather aspects" include Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Windspeed.
Please navigate to the Plots, Comparisons, and Data pages for an in-depth analysis and decide weather or not you need to take a rain check on your vacation this year.
